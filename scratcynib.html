About Us

Out of love and admiration to the rich and artistic practice of calligraphy and fonts, we have introduced “Scratcy Nib”, the revolutionary platform that allows all those passionate about this art to create personalized fonts and calligraphy arts.Through Scratchy Nib , individuals the opportunity to enjoy a personalized experience of developing their own fonts and to collaborate with others through means of social media. Corporates can have distinct identity through creating their own own font..

Creativity At every Inkdrop

At Scratchy Nib, we believe in the timeless beauty of handwritten artistry. Nestled at the intersection of tradition and creativity, our studio is dedicated to the exquisite art of calligraphy. We are more than just creators of beautiful letterforms; we are storytellers, transforming words into visual masterpieces.
